Consolidate the tables without losing the will to work

Sergio and his family love their business; they created Food for Health to produce the best natural cold-pressed olive oil in Switzerland.

We listen to nature without getting involved in its processes. We work with nature to use the most nutrient-rich ingredients it gives us. Science and nature work together to create ólixirTM products» - Sergio Panzarella.

Passionate about their business and their love, they ran their business in ordinary Excel spreadsheets as best they could. When Food for Health was distributing its product through a few shops and fairs, it was up to Sergio to handle the bookkeeping and stock tracking.

With an increasing number of counter-parties, turnover, warehouse rentals, it became almost unrealistic to consolidate those spreadsheets and it was time-consuming to find the right information.

Efficiency is a good relationship with the customers

The main challenge was dealing promptly with orders and searching for customer data.

I value the warm relationship I have developed with my regular clients; they are like friends to me and I cannot let them down. When a customer urgently needs a shipment to a restaurant, I do everything I can to ensure speedy delivery” says Sergio.

Improve and automate

We have an interesting history with LedgerPeek. We didn't start working after our first meeting. I had an experience of working with another company, which did not bring the expected results and left an unpleasant impressions. I came back to LedgerPeek and have been working with them for two years now.» - Sergio Panzarella, founder and CEO Food for Health.

The LedgerPeek team has created an inventory tracking system for Food for Health through Wyzio, a methodology for tracking payments received and billed, and implemented a methodology for cash flow Reporting&Management. They also handle my reporting and some administrative tasks.

LedgerPeek's friendly and patient professionals have allowed my startup to thrive and grow. Their services go beyond accounting and include optimising business processes that are essential for business development" - Sergio Panzarella, founder and CEO.
